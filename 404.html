Best Services

Electrical Services
  1. Installation of power and lighting systems, switchyards, motor control centers, high tension & Low tension cable laying works.
  2. Installation of project infrastructure systems including cable tray work, junction boxes, ladders tray, conduit etc.
  3. Procurement of hardware material such as cable tray, earthing work, steel etc. and material for Electrical distribution work.
  4. Installation of switch yard, bus duct, transformers, MCC panels (MV/LV), Lighting Distribution Panels.
  5. Installation and commissioning of power generation equipment.
  6. Motor Testing.
Instrument Services
  1. Installation of Transmitters, Switches & Gauges for Pressure, Temperature Flow and Level application, Control Valves, on / off valves, Breather valves, Safety Valves, panels, junction boxes, cable laying & termination, perforated cable tray work, pedestal fabrication.
  2. Installation of DCS and PLC systems, SCADA systems, hazardous area control systems, fire and gas systems and process installation, termination & commissioning of indicators, transmitters and controllers, valves & other fieldinstruments.
  3. Installation including cabling, marshalling panels, instrument air/gas tubing and impulse piping work.
  4. Testing, calibration & loop checking through fully trained technicians with certified testing equipment.
  5. Specialist knowledge on industrial projects for material selection and procurement related to electrical & instrumentation hardware material.
  6. Ability to pre-fabricate instrument support & other fabrication work for control packages on or off site.
  7. Supply of Manpower like skilled Engineers, Technicians for shut down maintenance, installation and commissioning activities on deputation
Mechanical Services
  1. Fabrication and Erection of Heavy structure, Steel plate, Vessels, Tank, Etc.
  2. Fabrication Erection and Testing of Piping (MS, SS, CS, HDPE)
  3. Equipment Installation
